About Carlyle Courtyard

Experience Anaheim living at Carlyle Courtyard. Carlyle features Studio Apartments with floorplan options. We offer either highly upgraded unfurnished units and traditional furnished units to best meet your needs. The highly upgraded Studio units includes flooring, granite countertops, appliances, LED recessed lighting, marble tile bathrooms, A/C, crown molding and other upgraded finishes throughout the units. You'll experience a great selection of outdoor amenities including a pool, onsite parking (covered and uncovered), fountains, open space, a landscaped courtyard, plantings, and large trees for your enjoyment. With close proximity to the 5 freeway, this property is ideal for those working in the city and nearby cities. Our professional leasing team is available to help you find your perfect new place. It's time to love where you live. Stop by for a visit today.

Carlyle Courtyard is an apartment community located in Orange County and the 92801 ZIP Code.

Neighborhood

Located between Downtown Anaheim and Garden Grove, South Anaheim puts you close to all of Anaheim's favorite features: Disneyland, Knott's Berry Farm, the Anaheim Convention Center, the Honda Center (home of the Anaheim Ducks), and the Angels Stadium. Living in South Anaheim provides endless opportunities to enjoy shopping, dining, nearby theaters, museums, and (of course) theme parks. The economy of the region is based on tourism, and Disney is the city's largest employer.

With the year-round warm climate and nearby beaches, South Anaheim apartments are ideal for those who enjoy outdoor activities like swimming, surfing, and boating. South Anaheim is dotted with hotels and resorts, providing a resort-like feel to the entire neighborhood. Apartments closer to the Santa Ana River provide easy access to the Santa Ana River Trail and Bikeway, a 30-mile, multi-use trail.
